Courage changes everything.

    Panamera Concept.

    Panamera


    Can a vision be logical? Is it even allowed to be? Probably not. We believe that the more visionary an idea at the start, the more exciting it is at the end. Then it's worth fighting for. That was the case with the Panamera. A sports car for four? With impressive performance and great comfort?

    With the dynamism typical of Porsche and simultaneously more efficient? Many said it was impossible. Others called it brave.

    The Design.

    Panamera Executive


    The Panamera Executive models combine sporty driving and comfortable working in the rear. The body has been lengthened by 15cm, which benefits the rear passengers in particular.

    Silver-coloured elements above the sideskirts are visual features of the Executive models. The air outlet trims in the front wings are in silver colour (high-gloss).
